About Logo

Concept

Our alumni logo is inspired from the letter 'M' which is already used in the existing logo of MCET.

The logo/identity is a combined depiction of flame, flower and continuity

The 'M' transforming into flame which symbolises power.
Why flower? Well, The beauty in a flower evokes unexpressed thoughts and feelings that are sometimes difficult to find words for, it also symbolises love, friendship, accomplishment and sympathy which perfectly solves the purpose of alumni association. Because we all have very good memories attached with MCET and we all want MCET to grow
